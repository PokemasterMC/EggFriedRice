* ~ ~ EGG FRIED RICE ~ ~ *



*Required Ingredients:-

  • Rice[6 cups]
  • Soy Sauce[4tbspoons]
  • Butter Or Oil[4tbspoons]
  • Eggs[4]
  • Cloves Of Minced Garlic[4]
  • Sliced Chilli Or Pepper[1-2]
  • Chopped Spring Onions[3-4]
  • Sliced Onion/s[1Large or 3Tiny]
  • Sliced Cabbage[1/3]
  • Wok
  • Rice Cooker
  • Turner
  • Measuring Spoon
  • Plate


*Spices[As Per Required Taste]:-

  • Black Pepper[Or White Pepper]
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • M.S.G


*Steps For Making Egg Fried Rice:-

  1. Cook the Rice in the Rice cooker at HEIGH Flame till 3 WHISTLES.[Making the rice DAY OLD is reccomended].
  2. Take your Wok and add Butter/Oil and heat at medium heat till butter melts and oil simmers.
  3. Now turn the heat up by a Pinch and add your Garlic,Chilli/Pepper,Onions and all the spices.
  4. After Some time{after the smell of spices becomes strong}add your Eggs and Scramble them with the turner.
  5. After the Scrambled Eggs have a Relatively Solid shape add your Rice and Soy Sauce.
  6. Wait for some time and then add the Spring Onions and after some time serve it on Plate.
  7. Add Sliced Cabbage and ENJOYY!!!!!!



ENJOY YOUR EGG FRIED RICE!!
